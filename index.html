Waveball game

This game was created during the Global Game Jam under a 48 hour limit. It's a two player game where each of the players holds one end of a rope. By moving the arm up and down a player can create waves that travel towards the other player. The objective is to push the ball over the other players head in order to get points. The player that first reaches 10 points wins.
